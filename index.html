PROJECT · 01
BRICS News Trend Data Pipeline
Mar 2025 – Jun 2025 · GuangZhou Broadcasting Network (International Communication Center)
Python Selenium MongoDB ETL
Problem

The International Communication Center needed to continuously monitor and analyze information trends across BRICS countries' major news outlets to support editorial strategy and cross-regional reporting decisions.

Data

Unstructured news article text scraped from major news websites of 5 BRICS member countries; structured metadata fields include source, publication date, topic tags, and region — stored in MongoDB collections.

Approach

Built automated data collection pipelines using Python Requests for static pages and Selenium for dynamic content. Implemented data cleaning routines and produced visual trend summaries for editorial review.

My Contribution

Independently designed and implemented the full scraping-to-storage pipeline. Sole engineer for this workflow — performed all data cleaning and produced editorial-ready visualizations.

Outcome / Impact

Delivered a structured, regularly updated news dataset enabling cross-regional trend analysis across 5 BRICS countries. Directly supported editorial decision-making and the station's international communication strategy.
